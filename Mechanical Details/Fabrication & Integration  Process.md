Fabrication And Macufacturing:
The shell of the object was 3D Printed to house all the components internally, excluding the IR Reciever
It houses the Battery, Buzzer, Slide Switch, OLED and the IR Emmiter.
Design Note:
The Game is designed to be scalable. Each Gun has a slot to put in the ESP32 (Controller of the gun) which is assigned its own ID and added to the network of ESP32's playing the game. This easy swap-ability and focus on the ESP32 being the core of the gun (making use of the RMT function) is a key insight behind the form of the gun aswell.
The visuals are inspired by Retail Guns. Model inspiration by Rell Vision
