# Material Note:
**Considerations-** Weight of batteries - It is placed at the bottom so that the weight of the gun remains heavy to the user, but it isn't top heavy, making it easy to still swing around when in action
**Sturdiness** (Didnt Work Out) - The shell must be sturdy to take the force of the grip of players running around and shooting.

# Fabrication And Macufacturing:
The shell of the object was 3D Printed to house all the components internally, excluding the IR Reciever. 3D Printing was used to deliver the precision required to place all the components in the preferred position in one cohesive shell. 
It houses the Battery, Buzzer, Slide Switch, OLED and the IR Emmiter.

# Assembly Steps:
[-] 3D Model the Shell
[-] 3D Print in 2 halves
[-] Join Halves
